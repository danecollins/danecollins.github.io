Shepherds Pie
====================
**Adapted from Cook's Illustrated**

Purchased  | Pantry Items
-----------  | ------------
1+ lb. ground beef      | 2 garlic cloves
1 medium onion          | 1 tsp Woscestershire sauce
16 oz peas and carrots  | 1 tbs tomato paste
2 large russet potatoes | 1 tsp Montreal steak
12 oz beef stock        | 1 tsp paprika
                        | 2 tbs + 2tbs roux


Timeline (95 min total)
-----------
* 60 minutes to prepare
* 35 minutes to bake




Steps
-----

1. **Cook potatoes** for 30 minutes
1. **Brown** onion and ground beef
2. **Add garlic** for last minute
2. **Mix in** remaining ingredients
3. **Salt and pepper** to taste
4. Make **gravy** and to ground beef
3. Add mix to pan
4. Cover with mashed potatoes
5. **Bake 35 minutes** at 350
