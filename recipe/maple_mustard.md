Maple Mustard Sauce
====================
**Internet**

Ingredients  | Pantry Items
-----------  | ------------
1 onion      | 1 cup broth
1/3 cup maple syrup | 3 tbs balsamic vinegar
                    | 3 tbs mustard


Steps
-----

1. **Brown** onions
2. **Mix in** remaining ingredients and reduce
