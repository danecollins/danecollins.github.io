Marsala Sauce
====================
**Home Chef**

Ingredients    | Pantry Items
-----------    | ------------
Parsley, fresh | 3/4 cups Dry Marsala
               | 3/4 cups concentrated chicken stock
               | 2 tbs butter/flour (roux)


Steps
-----

1. **Deglaze pan** with Marsala
2. **Add** chicken stock
3. **Reduce** to half volume
4. **Mix in** roux
5. **Add** Parsley



Notes
-----
* Can easily scale up
