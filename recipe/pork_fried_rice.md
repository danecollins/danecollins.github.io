# Pork Fried Rice
**Personal**


## Ingredients

| Purchased         | Pantry                           |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------- |
| 1 slice thick ham | 2 rice cooker cups prepared rice |
| 2 garlic cloves   | chili-garlic sauce               |
| 2 eggs            | soy sauce                        |
|                   | 1 cup peas                       |

## Steps

- Cook egg, add ham then garlic
- Pan fry rice, add soy and chili sauce
