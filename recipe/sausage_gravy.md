# Sausage Gravy

## Ingredients

| Purchased            | Pantry                |
| -------------------- | --------------------- |
| 1/2 lb sausage       | 1 tbs flour           |
| 1 cup milk           | sage, fennel, pepper  |


## Steps

1. Cook sausage
2. Add and brown flour in fat
3. Slowly add milk
4. Add sage, fennel, pepper, salt to taste
