# Ginger Hoisin Shrimp
---
**Cook's Illustrated**

Purchased           | Pantry Items
-----------         | ------------
1 lb shrimp         | 2 tbs hoisin
2 tbs grated ginger | 1 tbs rice vinegar
2 scallions         | 2 tbs soy sauce
6 oz noodles        | 2 tbs water

## Steps

1. **Toss** shrimp in sugar/salt/pepper mix
