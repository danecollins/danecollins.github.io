# Pancakes
---
**Cook's Illustrated**

Purchased    | Pantry Items
-----------  | ------------
             | 160g flour
             | 1 tbs sugar
             | 3.5 tsp (17g) baking powder
             | pinch salt
             | 1/2 tsp vanilla
             | 3 tbs butter
             | 10 oz milk
             | 1 egg

## Timeline (20 min total)
* 10 min prep and mix
* 10 min cook

## Steps

1. **Mix** dry ingredients and liquid ingredients separately
2. **Combine** ingredients
3. **Cook** pancakes on medium heat


## Notes
* Makes 5 large pancakes
