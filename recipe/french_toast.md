# French Toast
---
**Taste of Home**

Purchased     | Pantry Items
-----------   | ------------
1/2 cup cream | 1/2 cup milk
              | 2 egg yolks
              | 2 tbs brown sugar
              | 2 tsp vanilla
              | 1/2 tsp cinnamon
              | nutmeg




## Steps

1. Mix ingredients
2. Dredge bread
3. Cook



## Notes
* Don't dredge for too long as it gets hard to cook enough.
